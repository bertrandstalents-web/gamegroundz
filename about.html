Our Story

Built for the love
of the game.

We are building the single destination to discover, compare, and reserve sports facilities. Connecting athletes, organizers, and facility owners.

Our Mission

To eliminate the friction of organizing sports by making the discovery and booking of facilities as easy as showing up to play. Think marketplace meets sports community.

The Problem

Booking sports facilities today is stuck in the past. It's fragmented, frustrating, and incredibly inefficient.

  • Endless phone calls and unanswered emails.
  • Outdated spreadsheets and physical schedules.
  • Hidden fees and surprise charges.

Our Solution

We developed a seamless platform that connects you directly to available surfaces in real-time. No hassle, just sports.

  • Instant booking and confirmation.
  • Verified facilities with real photos and reviews.
  • Transparent pricing with absolutely zero hidden fees.
Who We Are

The Team Behind the Turf

We are a proudly bilingual, Quebec-based team of sports enthusiasts who got tired of the old way of doing things. From organizing our own beer league hockey to trying to find a turf field on a Sunday afternoon, we experienced the frustration firsthand.

GameGroundz wasn't built in a boardroom. It was conceptualized in the locker room, built by players, and designed for anyone who loves the game as much as we do. energetic, authentic, and driven by our community.

Our Core Pillars

The values that drive every decision we make.

Accessibility

We believe sports should be for everyone. By removing the barriers to booking space, we make it easier for people to get active and stay connected.

Transparency

What you see is what you pay. We don't believe in hidden fees or surprises. Honest pricing and honest availability, always.

Community

We aren't just software. We are athletes, organizers, and builders fighting to strengthen the sports community locally and globally.

Join the Movement

Whether you're looking to hit the ice, field, or court, or you manage a premier sports facility, we want you on our team.
