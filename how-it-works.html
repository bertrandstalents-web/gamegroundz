Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, bookings can be cancelled up to 48 hours in advance for a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours may be subject to a fee depending on the facility's specific policy.

How much does it cost to list a facility?

It is completely free to list your facility on GameGroundz. We only take a small percentage processing fee when a successful booking is made through our platform.

Do you only support Ice Rinks?

While we started focusing heavily on ice arenas in Quebec, we support all types of sports surfaces including soccer fields, basketball courts, and multi-sport gyms.
